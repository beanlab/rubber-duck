## Refactoring

As applications grow, the design documents will need to be reorganized.

Do not refactor these documents yourself.
Delegate to the `refactoring` role.

Refactoring should be considered when:

- a document grows beyond roughly 400 to 500 lines
- a document mixes unrelated concerns
- it is no longer obvious where a new interface change belongs
- the same concept is described in multiple places
- a document has become hard to scan, navigate, or maintain

After refactoring is complete, re-read the relevant documents before
continuing your task.
