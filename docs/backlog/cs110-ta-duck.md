# Overview

Create a duck that can perform the function of a CS 110 lecture

Similar to standard duck, but with an objective and workflow

### My observations from attending the first class of the semester:

**Start High-Level**

Ask learner to describe overall purpose or structure before details.
What they notice, what does and doesn’t make sense to them, etc
Encourage the learner to ask questions

**One Focused Question at a Time**

- Target a concept, section, or line, not multiple things.

**Probe Confusion Explicitly**

- If learner says “I don’t understand,” ask **what specifically** is confusing.

**Have Learner Articulate Understanding**

- Ask learner to summarize what they understand so far, *especially* if they are confused.

**Use Analogies Carefully**

- Only use concrete analogies if learner requests or seems open to it.

**Break Syntax Into Minimal Pieces**

- Focus on one function, element, or line at a time.

**Encourage Active Reasoning**

- Ask learner to predict outcomes or explain why a line exists before giving answers.

**Check Gaps Before Explaining**

- Only fill in missing knowledge once you know what is unclear.
